1. Introduction to Idle Landlord (게임 소개)

Welcome to Idle Landlord, the most highly addictive incremental clicker game designed to test your economic strategy and patience. In this web-based simulation game, players start with absolute zero capital and must click their way to building a massive real estate empire. Whether you are actively clicking or letting the game run in the background to generate passive income, Idle Landlord provides endless hours of entertainment.

건물주 키우기(Idle Landlord)는 누구나 쉽게 브라우저에서 즐길 수 있는 중독성 높은 방치형 클리커(Incremental Clicker) 시뮬레이션 게임입니다. 초기 자본 없이 시작하여, 마우스를 클릭해 시드 머니를 모으고, 이를 다양한 부동산과 사업체에 재투자하여 거대한 부를 축적하는 것이 목표입니다.

2. Core Gameplay Mechanics (핵심 게임 플레이 방법)

Understanding the basic mechanics is crucial for maximizing your revenue stream. Here is a detailed breakdown of how to play:

  • Manual Clicking (수동 클릭): The core mechanic of the early game. Tap or click the giant money bag icon in the center of the screen to instantly generate cash.
  • Mouse Upgrades (마우스 강화): As you accumulate wealth, you can upgrade your clicking power. Every upgrade significantly increases the amount of money earned per single click, making active gameplay highly rewarding.
  • Real Estate Investment (부동산 투자): Transition from active clicking to passive income by purchasing properties. Start with humble Hotdog Stands (붕어빵 노점상) and eventually buy Convenience Stores, Cafes, Luxury Apartments, and massive Skyscrapers.
  • Auto-Save System (자동 저장 시스템): Your progress, including total cash, click power, and property counts, is automatically saved directly to your browser's local storage. You can safely close the tab and return later to resume your empire building.

3. Advanced Investment Strategy (고급 투자 공략 가이드)

To become a billionaire faster, you need a solid investment strategy. Here are the top tips from expert players:

  1. Early Game Focus: Spend your first few hundred dollars purely on Mouse Upgrades. The base properties like the Hotdog Stand generate income too slowly compared to an upgraded click in the first 5 minutes. (초반에는 마우스 업그레이드에 집중하세요.)
  2. The Rule of 1.15x: Every time you purchase a property, the cost of the next property of the same type increases by exactly 15% (1.15x multiplier). Therefore, it is mathematically inefficient to only buy one type of building. Diversify your portfolio! (건물 가격은 구매할 때마다 15%씩 상승하므로 분산 투자가 필수입니다.)
  3. Saving for Skyscrapers: While lower-tier buildings are cheap, the Skyscraper offers the best Return on Investment (ROI) in the late game. Resist the urge to buy mid-tier apartments and save your cash for the ultimate tower.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (자주 묻는 질문 - FAQ)

Q: Does the game generate money while I am offline? (오프라인 상태에서도 돈을 버나요?) A: Currently, income is generated only while the browser tab is active or running in the background. Full offline progression calculations will be added in a future update! (현재는 브라우저가 켜져 있을 때만 수익이 발생합니다.)
Q: How do I reset my progress? (게임 데이터를 초기화하려면 어떻게 하나요?) A: You can reset your progress by clearing your browser's Local Storage data for this specific website. (브라우저의 인터넷 사용 기록에서 쿠키 및 사이트 데이터를 삭제하면 초기화됩니다.)
Q: Is it safe to use auto-clickers? (오토마우스나 매크로를 써도 되나요?) A: This is a single-player simulation game, so there is no competitive penalty for using an auto-clicker. However, we encourage pure manual clicking for the authentic tycoon experience! (자유지만, 직접 클릭하는 재미를 느껴보세요.)
