About the Game

1. What is Idle Landlord?

Idle Landlord is a highly addictive incremental clicker game where you start with empty pockets and build a real estate empire. Click the money bag to earn your first dollars, then invest your earnings into various businesses ranging from a simple Hotdog Stand to a massive Skyscraper.

2. How to Play & Tips

  • Click to Earn: Tap the giant money bag icon to instantly generate cash.
  • Mouse Upgrade: Increase your clicking power. Essential for fast early-game growth.
  • Invest in Real Estate: Buy properties to generate passive income every second. Your properties work for you even when you're not clicking!
  • Auto-Save: Your progress is automatically saved to your browser. You can close the page and come back later without losing your empire.
