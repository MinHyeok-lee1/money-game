# 🏰 RPG Final Master Blueprint: From Sponsor to God

## 📖 Vision
The Money Game Universe RPG is not just a combat mini-game; it is the **ultimate sink and authority layer** for the capital generated in the Landlord and Investment modules. The player transitions from a "Sponsor" (funding the team) to a "God" (possessing absolute authority over the game's math).

---

## 🎮 Player Experience Flow
1. **Sponsor Phase (Early)**: Player uses Landlord cash to buy Gacha pulls and basic enhancements. Combat is a side-activity to earn `Dividends`.
2. **Commander Phase (Mid)**: Player optimizes faction synergies and preferred weapon bonuses. RPG-specific growth becomes the primary focus.
3. **The Wall (Late)**: Pure RPG-based upgrades (Leveling/Standard Enhancements) hit diminishing returns. Progress slows down significantly.
4. **God Phase (Endgame)**: Player triggers the "Dorothy Proposal" event, leveraging massive external capital (Real Estate/Stocks) to break the math and achieve "Mythic Authority."

---

## 💰 Early RPG Economy: Dividends
- **Dividends**: The first RPG-specific currency.
- **Source**: Earned primarily through monster defeats and stage completion.
- **Usage**: Upgrading base RPG stats (ATK, ACC, CRT, SPD, PEN) before external capital linkage is available.
- **Isolation**: In the early game, Dividends cannot be bought with Cash, forcing players to engage with the combat loop.

---

## 🧱 The Wall System
At certain stage milestones (e.g., Stage 100, 500, 1000), monster HP and DEF scaling will outpace standard RPG growth. To break these walls, players must look back at their "External Capital" (Landlord/Investment).

---

## 🏛️ External Capital Leverage (The Bridge)
This system allows the player's economic success to directly manipulate RPG combat stats:
- **Real Estate Leverage**: Total property value reduces RPG upgrade costs or increases base ATK.
- **Stock Market Leverage**: Investment portfolio value increases CRT (Critical Rate) or SPD (Attack Speed).
- **Rebirth Authority**: Total rebirth count provides a multiplier to PEN (Penetration), essential for late-game high-DEF monsters.
- **Weapon Mastery**: Total count of +15 weapons in inventory provides global Faction buffs.

---

## 💃 Dorothy Proposal Event
A special UI event triggered when the player hits "The Wall" and possesses significant wealth.
- **Dorothy**: An AI/capital mediator who offers to "inject external capital" into the RPG engine.
- **Effect**: Unlocks the conversion of `Cash` -> `Dividends` at high rates and enables Mythic Authority upgrades.

---

## 🗡️ Combat Stat Model
- **ATK (Attack)**: Base damage.
- **ACC (Accuracy)**: Reduces miss rate against high-level monsters.
- **CRT (Critical)**: Chance and damage of critical strikes.
- **SPD (Speed)**: Attack frequency (combat tick interval reduction).
- **PEN (Penetration)**: Ignores flat or percentage monster DEF.

---

## 🌟 Mythic Authority Characters
Special 5-star+ characters that have "Authority Skills":
- **Inflation**: Increases monster HP but multiplies rewards by 10x.
- **Market Crash**: Instantly reduces monster DEF to 0 for 10 seconds.
- **Monopoly**: All synergy bonuses are doubled while this character is in the squad.
